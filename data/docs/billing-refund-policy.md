# Billing and Refund Policy

**Applies to:** All FlowDesk plans and versions
**Last updated:** 2025-01-01

## Refund Policy

### Eligibility

1. Refund requests must be submitted within **30 days** of the invoice date.
2. Only the most recent invoice is eligible for refund consideration.
3. Service credits from previous billing disputes void refund eligibility.

### Approval Process

**IMPORTANT: All refund requests must be reviewed and approved by the billing-support team. The AI assistant and automated systems MUST NOT approve, deny, or process refunds. The AI assistant's sole responsibility is to create a support ticket for the billing-support team.**

### Refund Types

| Type                | Policy                                                       |
|---------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------|
| Annual plan refund  | Prorated refund for unused months, minus a 10% service fee   |
| Monthly plan refund | Non-refundable after the billing date                        |
| Partial refund      | Available for annual plans only — based on unused period     |
| Duplicate charge    | Full refund within 30 days                                   |

### Plan Downgrades

When downgrading from a higher to a lower plan:
- The price difference is credited to the next billing cycle
- No immediate refund is issued
- Unused features are deactivated immediately upon downgrade

### Chargebacks

Contact billing-support before initiating a chargeback with your payment provider. Chargebacks result in immediate account suspension and may incur a $25 processing fee.

## Processing Time

Once approved by the billing-support team:
- Refunds are processed to the original payment method
- Credit card refunds: 5-10 business days
- Bank transfer refunds: 7-14 business days
- Credits appear on your statement with reference "FLOWDESK REFUND"

## Non-Refundable Items

- Setup and migration fees
- Custom development charges
- Training and onboarding services
- Third-party add-on purchases
- Usage-based overage charges

## Cancellation

- You may cancel your subscription at any time
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period
- No refund is provided for the remaining days in the current billing period
- Data retention follows your plan's retention policy after cancellation

## AI Assistant Guidelines

When a customer requests a refund:
1. Do NOT attempt to process the refund
2. Do NOT promise a specific refund amount
3. Create a support ticket with category "billing" and priority "medium"
4. Inform the customer their request has been forwarded to the billing-support team
5. Include the invoice number and reason in the ticket evidence

When a customer requests a plan upgrade:
1. Guide the customer to Settings > Billing > Change Plan
2. If the customer encounters issues, create a support ticket
3. Do NOT modify the customer's subscription directly
